Event Name : Yoga Classess

Event Date : 2nd August 2022

Event Description : VESIT Students Club in association with the VESIT Sports Council started Yoga classes on 2nd August 2022. The sessions were conducted by Mrs.Pallavi Pawar, who is not only a VESIT Alumni but also a certified Yoga Guru, having more than 10 years of experience. The session was conducted offline for the participants in Amphitheater, while Ms. Pallavi Pawar joined online on Google Meet. It was a half-hour session twice a week, which began at 7:30 am. This program continued for two months after which it closed down for exams and winter vacation.
