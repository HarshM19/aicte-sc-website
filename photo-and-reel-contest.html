VESIT STUDENTS CLUB

Event Name : Photo And Reel Contest

Event Date : 15 August 2022

Event Description : VESIT Students Club (under AICTE SPICE Scheme) in collaboration with VESIT PHOTOCIRCLE organized the 'Photo and Reels Contest’ on 15th August 2022, to celebrate the campaign ‘Har Ghar Tiranga’, under the aegis of Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav to encourage people to bring the Tiranga home and to hoist it to mark the 75th year of India’s Independence. The theme of the Reel Contest was 17 Sustainability Goals where the participants had the freedom to select their own choice of music and videography. The Photography Contest had the theme Har Ghar Tiranga, where they had to click photographs of themselves with the Tiranga and also proof of uploading it on the National portal. More than 30 students participated in both competitions. The prize money for the winner was ₹1500, and the 1st and 2nd runners-up received ₹1000 and ₹750 respectively.
